Support BotBridge.AI

Help keep our services free and accessible by contributing through donations or by partnering with us to host an API server.

1. Host an API Server

Support BotBridgeAI, one of the many third-party services powered by BotBridge, by partnering with us to host an API server. While BotBridge handles the technical infrastructure and backend operations, BotBridgeAI is dedicated to delivering unique services to users. By hosting a server, you help us enhance performance and availability for all users, ensuring that we maintain high-quality and accessible services across our platform.

This initiative is in the very early stages. If you're interested in partnering with us, please reach out to ##### either on our Discord or by email at hosting@BotBridgeai.net.

Your donations directly support the development of new features and improvements. Each tier comes with exclusive benefits:
