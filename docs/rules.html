Community Rules

Guidelines to keep BotBridge positive and welcoming.

Table of Contents

  • General Conduct
  • Content Guidelines
  • Prohibited Activities
  • Enforcement
  • Reporting Issues

General Conduct

We expect all community members to treat each other with respect, promoting a welcoming and friendly atmosphere for everyone.

Content Guidelines

Appropriate Content:

Content shared on BotBridge should be constructive, suitable for diverse audiences, and free of offensive material.

Attribution:

Credit original creators when sharing their work to promote respect and integrity.

Character Images:

All images uploaded should be respectful and align with our community standards. Any content deemed inappropriate will be removed.

Prohibited Activities

The following activities are not allowed on BotBridge:

  • Spamming or excessive self-promotion
  • Sharing personal information without consent
  • Using multiple accounts to evade restrictions
  • Creating characters that imitate real people deceptively
  • Engaging in inappropriate or harmful content creation

Enforcement

Violations may lead to warnings, temporary bans, or permanent removal from the community, depending on the severity of the issue.

Reporting Issues

If you encounter a violation, please contact our moderators at report@botbridge.net.
