Conduct Guidelines

General Conduct Violations

  • Minor Misconduct (e.g., disrespectful comments, mild harassment)
    • First offense: Warning
    • Second offense: 1-day to 3-day ban
    • Third offense: 1-week ban, with a warning that further issues may lead to a longer ban
  • Severe Misconduct (e.g., harassment, discrimination, bullying)
    • First offense: 1-week ban
    • Repeat offenses: 1-month ban, and if persistent, consider a 3-month ban

Content Guidelines Violations

  • Inappropriate Content (offensive, hateful, explicit material)
    • First offense: 3-day to 1-week ban, content removal
    • Second offense: 2-week ban
    • Third offense: 1-month ban, with a clear warning about further consequences
  • Failure to Provide Attribution
    • First offense: Warning, with a reminder to give proper credit
    • Repeat offenses: 1-day to 3-day ban
  • Violation of Character Image Rules (e.g., nude images)
    • First offense: 3-day ban, with image removal and a reminder of the rules
    • Second offense: 1-week ban
    • Third offense: 1-month ban

Posting Guidelines Violations

  • Failure to Tag Bots
    • First offense: Reminder (still no ban, but a gentle prompt to tag correctly in the future)
    • Repeat offenses: Continued reminders, but no ban unless other guidelines are violated

Prohibited Activities

  • Spamming or Excessive Self-Promotion
    • First offense: 1-day to 3-day ban
    • Second offense: 1-week ban
    • Third offense: 1-month ban
  • Sharing Personal Information Without Consent

  • Evading Restrictions (using multiple accounts)
    • Immediate permanent ban of all accounts upon discovery.
  • Creating Characters That Resemble Real People Deceptively
    • First offense: 1-week ban, with character removal and a warning
    • Second offense: 2-week to 1-month ban
  • Creating Bots Representing Minors
    • Immediate deactivation, with a warning and guidance on appropriate content
